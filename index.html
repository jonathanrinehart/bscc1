Frequently Asked Questions

How does therapy with Blue Sails work?

Therapy with Blue Sails is a streamlined, personalized experience aimed at providing you with the best possible support for your mental health journey. Here’s how it works:

  1. Initial Consultation: Start with a simple sign-up process on our website, where you’ll provide some basic information about yourself and what you’re looking for in therapy.
  2. Matching Process: Based on the information you provide, we match you with a therapist who meets your needs and preferences. Our matching is done with care to ensure a good fit, enhancing the therapeutic experience.
  3. Begin Your Journey: Once matched, you’ll schedule your first session at a time that suits you. Whether you choose telehealth or in-person sessions, our flexible options are designed to fit into your lifestyle. Your therapist will work with you to understand your goals, develop a personalized treatment plan, and start the journey toward achieving your desired outcomes.
  4. Ongoing Support: Throughout your therapy process, you’ll have the support and guidance of your therapist. With regular sessions, you’ll work through challenges, track your progress, and make adjustments to your treatment plan as needed, ensuring that you’re always moving forward in your mental health journey.

By integrating clinically-proven therapy methods with the flexibility of in-person and telehealth options, Blue Sails provides a supportive, accessible, and effective path to mental wellness tailored just for you.

Can telehealth therapy really be as effective as in-person sessions?

Absolutely. Our telehealth sessions are designed to offer the same level of effectiveness as in-person therapy, backed by research and positive outcomes from our clients. Utilizing the latest in digital communication technology, we ensure a secure, confidential, and engaging environment that supports deep and meaningful therapeutic work. However, if you prefer in-person therapy, we offer in-person sessions as well.  Whether you choose telehealth or in-person sessions, you’re receiving personalized, high-quality care tailored to your needs.

How do I get matched with a therapist?

Our matching process is designed with your needs and preferences at the forefront. After you complete a brief intake form detailing your therapy goals, preferences, and any specific concerns, our system, supported by our team of mental health professionals, carefully selects a therapist who best matches your requirements. This tailored approach ensures that you are paired with a therapist whose expertise and approach align with your mental health journey, fostering a supportive and effective therapeutic relationship from the start.

I’ve had difficulty connecting with therapists in the past. How can I ensure I find the right match with your service?

Understanding the importance of a therapeutic match, we’ve developed a detailed matching process that considers your specific needs, preferences, and therapeutic goals. If at any point you feel the match isn’t right, we’re committed to working with you to find a therapist who better aligns with your needs, ensuring a supportive and productive therapeutic relationship.

How do you make therapy affordable and what about insurance coverage?

We strive to make therapy accessible and affordable. Our pricing is transparent, and we offer a range of payment options, including insurance coverage and sliding scale fees for those who qualify. Our team is here to help you navigate payment and insurance options, making sure you can access the care you need without undue financial stress.

How do you guarantee privacy during online therapy sessions?

We prioritize your privacy and confidentiality using HIPAA-compliant platforms for all our online sessions. These platforms feature end-to-end encryption, ensuring that your conversations and data are secure. Additionally, our therapists adhere to strict privacy policies, ensuring that everything discussed remains confidential.

I have a busy schedule. How can I fit therapy into my life?

We understand the demands of modern life, which is why we offer flexible scheduling, including evening and weekend appointments, through both our in-person and telehealth services. This flexibility allows you to integrate therapy into your life without having to compromise on your commitments.

I’m not sure if therapy is right for me. How can I make this decision?

Deciding to start therapy is a personal choice that comes from recognizing a need for support. We offer initial consultations to help you understand how therapy can benefit you. This no-obligation conversation allows you to explore your feelings and concerns with a professional, helping you make an informed decision about starting your therapy journey.

I’ve been disappointed by therapy before. How can you ensure a different experience?

We’re truly sorry to hear about your past experiences. Our approach is centered on understanding and meeting your unique needs, with a strong emphasis on personalization and respect. We believe in building a therapeutic relationship based on trust, empathy, and professionalism. Give us a chance to provide a different, positive experience that’s focused on your well-being.

When can I expect to see results from therapy?

The therapy journey is unique to each individual, and progress can vary. We focus on setting realistic goals and continuously tailor our approach based on your feedback and evolving needs. While some clients notice improvements early on, we encourage patience and commitment to the process for lasting change.

What is the wait time to start therapy after signing up?

We aim to minimize wait times as much as possible. Our team works diligently to match you with a therapist quickly, often within a few days of completing your intake form. Our goal is to ensure timely access to care so you can start your journey toward better mental health without unnecessary delays.

I feel uncomfortable talking about personal issues. How do you create a comfortable environment for therapy?

It’s natural to feel hesitant about opening up. Our therapists are skilled at creating a safe, welcoming, and non-judgmental space that encourages sharing at your own pace. We emphasize building a strong therapeutic alliance where you feel understood and supported, making it easier to discuss and work through personal issues.
